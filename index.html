    • Availability Calendar

  • How to Update my Calendar

    1. Select a status
    2. Double click on the Calendar.
    3. Drag through the slots that you will be in that status.

  • How the calendar affect the event

    The change you made in your calendar would be updated in the Participants' availability table in the event detail page.
    To protect privacy we did not expose individuals availability. Therefore, the change made by you may not be that obviously
    To test this function, you can create an event only for you and in that case, the change caused by your update would be obvious.
